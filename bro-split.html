Bro Split

One Muscle Group Per Day — 5 days per week

The Bro Split is one of the most classic bodybuilding programmes — one major muscle group per day, five days a week. While newer research favours higher frequency, the Bro Split still produces excellent results for intermediate to advanced lifters who can bring high intensity to each session. Each muscle gets hammered once a week with maximum volume, then gets seven full days to recover and grow. If you enjoy dedicated, focused sessions with high pump work, this is for you.

Programming Tips

  • The Bro Split shines when each session is trained with maximum intensity — low-effort sessions waste the once-weekly frequency.
  • Rest days fall on Saturday and Sunday, or you can swap Wednesday and Thursday to avoid three hard days in a row.
  • This split is not ideal for beginners — full body or upper/lower provides better skill practice for new lifters.
  • Track your lifts session to session and aim to beat your previous performance on the main compound each week.
