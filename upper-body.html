Upper Body Split

Chest · Back · Shoulders · Arms — 2 days per week

The Upper Body Split trains all upper body muscles in a single session, pairing push and pull movements to keep the workout balanced and time-efficient. This approach works well in an Upper/Lower or Upper/Lower/Full-Body structure, making it one of the most beginner-friendly and sustainable splits available. Each session hits chest, back, shoulders, and arms with enough volume to stimulate growth without excessive fatigue.

Programming Tips

  • Pair Upper A with Lower A, and Upper B with Lower B for balanced weekly programming.
  • Superset push and pull movements (e.g. bench + row) to save time without sacrificing quality.
  • Upper B uses higher reps and more isolation work — don't rush it; focus on feeling the muscle.
  • This split is ideal if you can train 4 days per week consistently.
