Pull Split

Back · Biceps · Rear Delts — 3 days per week

The Pull Split trains all muscles responsible for pulling movements — the back, biceps, and rear delts. Pairing these groups keeps session length efficient and allows full recovery before your next push day. This split builds the wide, thick back that creates an athletic physique, while developing bicep peak and rear shoulder health to balance your pressing volume.

Programming Tips

  • Prioritise feeling the back work — reduce weight if you're pulling with your arms only.
  • Alternate between Day A and Day B each week to ensure balanced thickness and width development.
  • Face pulls are non-negotiable — they protect your rotator cuff and balance shoulder health.
  • Bicep work comes last; they're already fatigued from rows and pulldowns.
